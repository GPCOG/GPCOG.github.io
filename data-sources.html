GIS Data

Here are sources for GIS data.

General

The Maine GeoLibrary is the primary source for GIS data in Maine. Maine GeoLibary Services include basemaps and geocoding services.

Census Cartographic Boundaries can be used to map Census data at various levels of geography.

Natural Earth is a public domain map dataset available at 1:10m, 1:50m, and 1:110 million scales.

Global Administrative Boundaries provides maps and spatial data for all countries and their subdivisions.

MaineDOT Map Viewer

The MaineDOT Public Map Viewer is the primary source for transportation data. This map viewer also includes some environmental and historical data.

To download data: click on the Layers menu on the right top side of the screen. Click the Add button, then click Show All to add more layers. Once you're done adding layers you can click on the the wrench icon next to the layer, select View Data, then click the save icon at the top of the attribute table to download the data.
